Premium class production absorbed the charm and the wisdom of the Eastern culture.

LAMAR — gifts and accessories for those who desire for the luxury and admire for the beauty.
High quality of each item emphasizes its exclusivity, as well as the status of its owner talks about his uniqueness.

In LAMAR, each product has no analogues in the world. All of them are defined as the masterpieces which carry the oriental traditions of the past. An exclusive dinnerware, swords, daggers, the chess — all of these are made of noble metals, exclusive wood species and gems.

LAMAR — “deluxe” gifts for your business partner, friend or family member. Every detail, from the material to the concrete size, is personally discussed with the client. The reason why we do it – because the East — is primarily a subtlety of mind and every little thing always carries deeper meaning than it seems.

This is why our customers are willing to pay for the excellent quality and unique design commensurate with the price of the premium class production.
